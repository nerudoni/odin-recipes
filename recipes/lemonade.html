Lemonade

home page

Description

This lemonade will leave you feeling refreshed and full of vigor

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup lemon juice

Steps

  1. Mix together water and sugar and boil over heat for a few mins
  2. Add in lemon juice and gradually add in the rest of the water
  3. Serve and enjoy
