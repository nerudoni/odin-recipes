Crispy White Bread

home page

Description

This bread is the most delicious bread you'll ever eat, it's a treat to your eyes, mouth and nose

Ingredients

  • 6 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespons yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons oil or margerine
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Steps

  1. Mix together the water, yeast and sugar and leave to rest
  2. Add margerine/oil and milk to the mixture
  3. In another bowl, mix the flour and salt together, leaving a well in the middle
  4. Add the wet mixture to the well and slowly mix together until a loose dough is formed
  5. Knead until dough is soft and supple
  6. Leave to rise for 1 hour
  7. Knead the dough and seperate into loaves
  8. Grease the pans and leave the dough to rise for 15 mins
  9. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius
  10. Place the dough into the oven for 30 mins
  11. Cool on a rack and serve
