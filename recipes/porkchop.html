Tonkatsu Porkchop

home page

Description

Tonkatsu porkchop is a delicious, quick and easy recipe which you could make for lunch or dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 Porkchops
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Flatten the porkchops with a meat tenderizer or your knife
  2. Coat the porkchops in the spices
  3. Place the spiced porkchops in the flour the the milk, repeat if you want a thick coating, and then the breadcrumbs
  4. Heat the pan with the oil to medium heat
  5. Fry until golden brown
  6. Place on a rack to cool for 5 mins and serve with rice
